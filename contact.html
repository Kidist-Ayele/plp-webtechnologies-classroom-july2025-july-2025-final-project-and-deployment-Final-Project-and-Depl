Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! We highly recommend making reservations, especially for dinner and weekends. You can call us or use our online reservation form above.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Many of our traditional dishes are naturally vegetarian. Please let us know about any allergies when making your reservation.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have street parking available on Bole Road, and the area is easily accessible by public transport.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We can accommodate private parties and events, including traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremonies. Please contact us at events@habeshakitchen.com for more information.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 2 hours notice for cancellations. For large parties (6+ guests), we require 24 hours notice.

Do you offer takeout?

Yes! We offer takeout for most of our traditional Ethiopian dishes. Please call ahead to place your order.
